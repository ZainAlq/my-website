FAQ

Frequently asked
questions

What is Sportify?
Sportify is Jordan's first corporate wellness network. It gives employees unlimited access to a curated network of gyms and fitness studios across Amman — through a single monthly subscription co-funded by their employer. Companies sign one contract, employees get instant app access, and gyms receive a steady new stream of members.
How can my company offer Sportify to employees?
Simply contact us via email or WhatsApp. We'll send a short onboarding form, agree on your plan and co-funding split, and get you set up within days. Your employees then receive a unique registration link to activate access immediately.
Can I use Sportify if my employer doesn't offer it?
Sportify is currently available exclusively as a company benefit. You can recommend it to your HR manager — just share our website and we'll handle the rest. We'll reach out to set things up on their end.
What does Sportify cost?
Plans start at 25 JOD/employee/month (Starter) and 30 JOD (Pro). The cost is co-funded — company covers 60–70%, employee contributes 30–40% via payroll. Enterprise pricing is available for 100+ employees. No setup fees, no lock-in.
Which gyms are in the network?
Current partners include Hive Fit Nation, Gold's Gym Jordan, Rizeup Sports, Magma Lifestyle, VY Health Club, My Tribe Gym, and more across Amman. The network grows regularly — new gyms added monthly.
How does the QR check-in work?
Open the Sportify app at the gym, tap the QR icon, and a unique check-in code is generated for the front desk to scan. No physical card needed — just your phone. Every check-in appears in your employer's monthly usage report.
